How It Works

1

Browse the Library

Use the sidebar to filter by Tools or Games, or press / to search by name, skill, or tag. Every activity card shows its category, difficulty level, and a short description.

2

Launch & Customise

Hit Launch on any card — the activity opens in a workspace tab. Most games have a Setup panel where you enter your own vocabulary, sentences, or images to match your lesson topic.

3

Manage Your Workspace

Run multiple tabs simultaneously — switch between a timer and a quiz without closing either. Use Split Screen for side-by-side activities, or Focus Mode to hide the side panel for full-screen projection.

4

Pin, Save & Return

Pin your favourite activities so they're always at the top. Your recent launches, open tabs, and custom data are saved automatically — come back tomorrow and everything is still there.

Platform Features

Dark Mode

Switch to a projector-friendly dark theme with one click. Your preference is saved and applied globally across all activities.

Split Screen

View two activities side-by-side — for example, a scoreboard alongside a quiz game, or a timer next to flashcards.

Focus Mode

Hide the side panel for a clean, full-screen experience. Perfect for projecting one activity to the entire class without distractions.

Auto-Save

Your open tabs, pinned activities, vocabulary inputs, and tool settings are saved to the browser automatically — no account needed.
