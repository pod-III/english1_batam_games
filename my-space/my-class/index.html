Gibbs' Reflection

1

Description

What happened? Describe the situation objectively — who, what, where, when.

2

Feelings

What were you thinking and feeling? How did students react?

3

Evaluation

What went well? What didn't go so well?

4

Analysis

Why did things go well or poorly? What sense can you make of the situation?

5

Conclusion

What have you learned? What could you have done differently?

6

Action Plan

What will you do differently next time? What concrete steps will you take?
