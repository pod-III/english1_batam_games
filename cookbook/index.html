Mini Apple Crumble

Thanksgiving Cooking Class • Level: Beginner

Prep 15m
Bake 25m
Temp 180°C

Loadout (Ingredients)

The Filling (Base)

  • 1 Apple (Fuji)
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • A pinch of Cinnamon

The Crumble (Top)

  • 2 Tbsp Flour
  • 1 and a half Tbsp Cold Butter
  • 1 Tbsp Sugar
  • 1 Tbsp Oats

Vocab Check

  • Dice (v): To cut into small cubes.
  • Rub-in (v): To mix butter into flour using fingertips until it feels like sand.
  • Sprinkle (v): To scatter small pieces or powder over food.
1

Dice the Apple

Wash your apple. Use the plastic knife to dice the apple into small cubes. Do not peel the skin (it has vitamins!).

Action: Put cubes in the foil cup. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
2

The Dry Mix

In your mixing bowl, combine the Flour, Sugar, and Oats.

Don't add the butter yet!

3

The Rub-In Technique

Add the Cold Butter. Use your fingertips to rub the butter into the flour mix.

Pro Tip: Keep the butter cold! If it melts, your crumble won't be crunchy. Stop when it looks like wet sand.
4

Assemble & Deploy

Layer the apples on the bottom of the cup and sprinkle a layer of sugar and cinnamon. Pour your "sand" mixture over the apples in the cup. Make sure the apples are covered.

Final Step: Hand it to the teacher. We bake for 25-30 minutes at 180°C.
