This hub is a desktop-optimized, high-performance web application designed for projector-based teaching. It consolidates **32 interactive activities** into a single interface, utilizing a "Chunky" design aesthetic that is both visually engaging for young learners and highly legible for those in the back of the classroom.

* **Teacher Console:** Many games feature a hidden or integrated console for manual word input, allowing real-time customization based on the day's lesson.

## üéÆ Activity Categories

### 1. Classroom Management & Tools
* **Class Tally:** A persistent behavior tracker for monitoring student participation and discipline.
* **Scoreboard:** Visual team-based scoring with large, tactile buttons.
* **Clock:** Multi-mode timekeeper with Clock, Timer, Stopwatch, and Calendar.
* **Team Picker & Task Assigner:** Randomized tools to ensure fairness during group work.
* **Lesson Notes:** Local-storage based notepad for quick lesson planning.
* **Whiteboard:** Digital drawing surface for quick explanations.
* **Slides:** Simple text-based presentation tool for on-the-fly lecturing.

### 2. Vocabulary & Spelling
* **Word Flashcards:** Digital drill cards with customizable categories.
* **Flashcard Maker:** Design and print custom A4 flashcards with cutting guides.
* **Hangman & Word Scrambler:** Classic spelling games with a modern twist.
* **Word Search & Crossword:** Auto-generating puzzles based on lesson vocabulary.
* **Unscramble:** Sentence and word-level syntax practice.
* **Reveal Pic:** Image guessing game where tiles are removed one by one.
* **Word Display:** Flashcard-style single word display for focus drills.

### 3. Speaking, Interaction & Movement
* **Hot Seat:** The ultimate speaking game for guessing vocabulary through descriptions.
* **Role & Speak:** Dice-rolling game combining chance with sentence building.
* **This or That:** Physical movement game where students choose sides.
* **Freeze Dance:** Music-based movement activity for energy breaks.
* **Story Dice & Ordering:** Creative writing and logical sequencing prompts.
* **Emoji Game:** Modern visual-to-word association challenges.

### 4. Logic, Strategy & Focus
* **Find the Ball (Magic Cups):** A high-stakes concentration game.
* **Card Match:** Memory-matching game for vocabulary retention.
* **Connects (Tic-Tac-Toe):** Classic strategy game for turning downtime into playtime.
* **Secret Code:** Logic-based decoding activities for advanced learners.
* **Spin Wheel & Shuffle:** Universal randomizers for selecting students or rewards.

## üõ† Technical Specifications
* **Framework:** Tailwind CSS for rapid UI development and "Neo-brutalism" styling.
* **Core Logic:** Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+) for maximum compatibility and zero-dependency speed.
* **Icons:** Lucide React / SVG icons for crisp scaling on 4K projectors.
* **Storage:** Uses `localStorage` to keep lesson notes, scores, and tally data persistent between browser refreshes.
